Task 1,3

  1. Gather all the ingredients you need for your sandwich.
    • Lay them out on a clean surface such as your kitchen counter or island.
  2. On one or both slices of bread, spread your desired condiments.
    • Common choices include mayonnaise, mustard, butter, or ketchup.
  3. Add vegetables of your choice on one of the bread slices.
    • Add your chosen meat if you want to have a non-vegetarian bite.
  4. To add creaminess and extra flavour, you can add your preferred type of cheese.
  5. If you like, you can cut your sandwich in half diagonally for a classic presentation.
    • Or use an electric sandwich maker or grill to make it more delicious.
